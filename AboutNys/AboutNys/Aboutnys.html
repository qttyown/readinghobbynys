Getting know more about me!

Hello! My passion for reading began at a young age with comic books like Impian Amani by Ana Muslim, which I loved so much that I would buy a copy whenever I found one. During my teenage years, I moved on to short novels such as Young Aisyah by Saridah Hamid Reja, further deepening my interest. In Form 4, I discovered online novels through Instagram, leading me to platforms like Wattpad. Since then, I’ve been actively reading digital stories and buying e-books from online sellers. This discovery greatly strengthened my love for reading and continues to inspire me.
