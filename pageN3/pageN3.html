Her Protector

by oniriquefairytale

☆ ☆ ☆ ☆ ☆

🧚‍♀️ SYNOPSIS 🧚‍♀️

Arash Zayden, salah seorang anggota unit pasukan khas Polis Diraja Malaysia. Pulangnya ke tanah air tanpa sebarang niat untuk menaruh rasa pada sesiapa pun termasuklah pada bekas teman wanitanya. Takdir yang disusun dari awal, titik sentuh pada garis masa yang bersilang dengan seorang gadis mengubah segalanya.

Daisha Sofea, gadis yang tidak beruntuk soal kasih sayang. Setelah perginya insan yang bergelar ibu, dia hilang individu yang menjadi tiang kekuatannya. Layak ke bagi Daisha Sofea untuk memberi peluang pada lelaki lain sedangkan atas sebab lelaki jugalah, hidupnya penuh dengan parut.

🌻 MY REVIEW 🌻

I really loved this story so much that I reread it so many times and never got bored. The plot follows Daisha Sofea, who was abandoned by her own father due to some misunderstanding, forcing her to live independently when a father should’ve been the one guiding her. Her meeting with Arash Zayden slowly brought light into her dark, quiet world. For me, Arash is the most “green flag” male character I’ve ever read. He helped Sofea through her darkest moments and never left her alone during her depression.

This story also highlights mental health, which I rarely see in online novels because it's such a sensitive topic and requires deep understanding about mental from the author. I’d rate it 9.8/10 because the storyline is not too heavy, not too light that i think reader can read it without worry about boring love plot and there are many scenes related to mental health that made me realize that even someone who seems cheerful may be silently struggling with their mental health.
